\section{Single-Path Selection: Reward Profiles}
\label{app:p1profiles}

The conditional path-selection agent is trained and evaluated across the reward-weight vectors
$\mathbf{w} = (w_1, w_2, w_3, w_4, w_{\text{probe}})$ of \cref{eq:p1reward}, spanning distinct application
intents. \Cref{tab:app:profiles} lists representative profiles.
